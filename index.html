About the Author

What Is palliative and Hospice Care?

Palliative care improves the quality of life for those with serious illnesses through pain relief and holistic support. Hospice care, a subset of palliative care, focuses on providing comfort and support to terminally ill individuals during their final stages of life.

What Are the roles the PHC teams play?

The roles of palliative and hospice care providers include assessing and managing symptoms, creating individualized care plans, offering emotional and psychological support, coordinating care, and providing end-of-life planning and grief support.

    What Did We to Help Them Release Stress?

    Dance Movement Therapy

    Dance Movement Therapy (DMT), also known as Dance/Movement Psychotherapy (DMP), is a form of expressive therapy that uses movement and dance to promote emotional, cognitive, physical, and social integration of individuals. It is a holistic approach that recognizes the interconnectedness of mind and body and aims to improve overall well-being.
